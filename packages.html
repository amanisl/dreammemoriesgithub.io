OUR PACKAGES

Modern Wedding Stories for the Discerning Couple

Budgeting for wedding photography and cinematography depends on the size and type of your wedding. Get in touch with us, and we will customise a photography and
cinematography package that is based on how much or how little you need. We typically offer the following types of packages. .

Typical Wedding Packages..

2 Day Wedding Packages

Our two day wedding packages will cover one pre-function (like a Mehendi or a Sangeet) and the main wedding day. Typical deliverables a full set of 800+ fully edited wedding pictures that form your main wedding story,additional images of friends and family, a wedding film with a short teaser and optionally additional wedding albums.

Additional Days

You can always add additional days to your wedding photography package that
includes smaller pre-functions or even post wedding events like a separate reception.

Rokas / Engagements

Getting hitched, but not sure about your wedding date yet? We’d love to start our journey with you right from the beginning! Our Roka packages include photography and a video, as well as a coffee table book from your day!

Pre-Wedding Shoots

Not set a date yet but want to create a fun, memorable album of memories with your significant other? We’re here to help! Our pre-wedding sessions can be as short as a few hours to multiple days spanning different locations across the world!
